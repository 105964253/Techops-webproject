Junior IT Support Specialist

Ref: 2025A

Salary: $45,000 - $55,000 per year

Reports to: IT Manager

Job Description

We are looking for a Junior IT Support Specialist to provide technical assistance to our clients and staff. You will help install, maintain, and troubleshoot hardware and software issues.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Provide technical support to employees and clients
  2. Install and configure computer hardware and software
  3. Monitor and maintain computer systems and networks
  4. Respond to IT support tickets in a timely manner
  5. Set up accounts for new users
  6. Train staff on basic computer skills and new software

Requirements

Essential:

  • Basic knowledge of computer systems and networks
  • Good problem-solving skills
  • Ability to explain technical issues clearly
  • Willingness to learn new technologies

Preferable:

  • College coursework in IT or related field
  • 6+ months of IT support experience
  • Basic certification (e.g., CompTIA A+)

About This Position

This is an excellent entry-level opportunity for someone looking to start their career in IT. We provide training and mentorship to help you grow your skills.
